Data Enablement Experience

Banking : Technical Lead

Pieter was appointed the role of Technical Lead, Architect and ETL developer for a data-warehouse consolidating corporate real estate data. The goal of this project was to calculate the total cost of ownership of the bank's real estate portfolio on a building level. He designed and implemented the architecture, the model and ETL processes consolidating 4 different systems and providing management with valuable insights in their asset portfolio. The entire build and processing was done using SQL database scripts.

Insurance : Team Lead

Pieter was responsible for the technical design and project roll-out of a Data Integration Layer using Microsoft Azure. A data integration project, fulfilling various requirements of which IFRS. The scope of the platform was later expanded to include data integration requirements from the entire organization, and make the data available for consumption by others.

Pharma R&D : Informatica Developer

Pieter worked for a large Pharmaceutical company in the R&D department as an ETL developer/analyst. The aim of the project was to integrate different systems into one consolidated, operational warehouse used for Clinical Trials Analytics. The Data Vault and Kimball methodology were used in different stages of the warehouse with focus to Data Quality and Compliance. Later it was decided to migrate towards a new Teradata platform where he continued his development work. Later he also became responsible for change and release management of the program.
